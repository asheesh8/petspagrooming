About the spa

Grooming that treats your pet like family.

At Pet Spa & Grooming, every visit is built around your pet's comfort. We take the time to keep things calm and unhurried, so even nervous pups and particular cats can relax through their groom.

Whether it's a quick freshen-up or a full head-to-tail makeover, our goal is the same: a clean, healthy, happy companion you can't wait to show off.

  • Patient, gentle handling for anxious and senior pets
  • Clean, comfortable studio in the heart of Essex Junction
  • A loyal local following with a 4.8-star reputation

What we offer

Grooming services for every coat & companion

Common services for dogs and cats. Call us for the full menu, breed-specific styling, and pricing tailored to your pet.

Bath & Brush

A thorough wash, blow-dry, and brush-out that leaves coats soft, clean, and tangle-free.

Full Grooming

Bath, haircut, and styling done to suit your pet's breed, coat, and your preferences.

Nail Trim & Tidy-Up

Quick, stress-free nail trims plus paw, face, and sanitary trims between full grooms.

De-Shedding

Deep-cleaning treatments that loosen and remove loose undercoat to cut down on shedding.

Ear & Hygiene Care

Gentle ear cleaning and hygiene trims to keep your pet fresh and comfortable.

Cat Grooming

Calm, careful grooming for feline friends — baths, brush-outs, and tidy trims.
